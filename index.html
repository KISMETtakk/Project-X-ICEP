To inspire and drive meaningful change by showcasing and supporting transformative projects such as the Tshwane Varsity Hackathon, Grade R Centre of Excellence, and Ndumo Community Project. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, enhancing educational opportunities, and empowering communities through collaboration and impactful initiatives.
To create a world where innovation and education converge to uplift communities and ignite progress. By celebrating and scaling successful projects from the Tshwane Varsity Hackathon, advancing early childhood education through the Grade R Centre of Excellence, and supporting sustainable development in the Ndumo Community Project, we envision a future where every individual has the tools and opportunities to thrive.

What We Do?

We stand for empowering people

Community Work

The Ndumo Community Project enhances well-being through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, including learning centers, health services, vocational training, and infrastructure improvements. It also promotes environmental sustainability, cultural enrichment, and technological advancement, while fostering community partnerships and volunteer involvement."

Empowering

The Tshwane Varsity Hackathon empowers students with hands-on coding experience and teamwork on real-world challenges, fostering innovation and creativity. It provides valuable networking opportunities and skill development, enhancing participants' career prospects and confidence

Care

The Grade R Centre of Excellence delivers high-quality early childhood education with a focus on holistic development through play-based learning. It provides individualized attention and fosters parental involvement to support children’s literacy, numeracy, and emotional growth before primary school.
